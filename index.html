Frequently Asked Questions

  • How many years of marketing experience do you have?
    I have over 6 years of full time marketing experience.
  • What industries have you worked with?
    Industries of worked for include: mortgage, real estate, industrial engineering, military hardware & software, travel, horticulture/CBD, healthcare, and human services.
  • What type of marketing is right for my business?
    This question is specific to your industry. There are various types of marketing, and you need to encompass as many types as possible to keep the wheel moving. Reach out if you have any specific questions you’d like me to answer!
  • Do I need a blog?
    Short answer: yes. Blogs are powerful in gaining quality traffic. When people are actively looking for what you’re selling/offering, the blog will help drive your target audience to your website. Blogs also are a great way to showcase your expertise within your industry, and also increase your brand awareness.
  • How can I transform more leads into customers?
    You should first generate quality leads by investing your efforts into the right channels. Find a target audience that will buy from you and set up Google Ads with the right keywords. That’s a great first start. You then want to leverage customer feedback. Take to heart what they say about your product and adjust your website/campaigns/social outlets accordingly. A good question to keep in mind is “What made my customers buy from me? Are they satisfied? How can I improve my offer based off of their experience?” Lastly, pay attention to your data!
